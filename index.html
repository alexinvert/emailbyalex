ABOUT ME

I am a full-time email designer and developer based in Brighton, UK with a penchant for all things pixel and type. I specialise in crafting beautiful, solid, custom responsive HTML email campaigns and templates with focus on accesability in both dark and light modes. Some of my past clients include Tesco, Secret Escapes, Nuffield health, Prezzo and Justgiving.

I've been in the field for 14 years and have loved every minute of it. I'm a designer, developer and overall creative thinker.
